  # Privacy Policy

  **Effective date:** June 1, 2026

  GradientForge ("the App") is a macOS application developed by Thomas Crampton. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information
  when you use the App.

  **In short: we do not collect any personal information.** The App runs entirely on your Mac. None of your data leaves your device.

  ## What we collect
  
  **Nothing.** GradientForge does not collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal information, usage data, or content from your device.

  Specifically, the App does not:

  - Require an account or sign-in
  - Connect to any server we control or operate
  - Include analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting
  - Include advertising or advertising identifiers
  - Use third-party tracking technologies
  - Read or transmit your contacts, photos, location, or other personal data

  ## Information you create or import
  
  Any datasets, trained models, exported weights, Core ML files, and other content you create, edit, import, or export remain stored locally on your Mac. They are saved to:

  - The App's sandboxed Application Support folder (for custom datasets you save inside the app)
  - Locations you explicitly choose (when you use Export commands)

  We never see, receive, or have access to any of this content.

  ## Network activity

  GradientForge does not initiate any network connections. macOS itself may perform background activities — such as verifying the App's code signature or checking the Mac App Store
  for updates — but these are managed by macOS and Apple, not by us, and are governed by Apple's own privacy practices.

  ## If you contact us for support

  If you choose to email us at the address below for support, bug reports, or feature requests, your message and email address will be used only to respond to your request. We do not
   add support contacts to a mailing list, share them with third parties, or use them for any other purpose. You can request deletion of your message at any time.

  ## Children's privacy

  GradientForge is suitable for users of all ages, including educational use. Because the App does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect
  data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in other jurisdictions). The App is compliant with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar
  laws worldwide.
  
  ## Your rights under GDPR, CCPA, and similar laws

  Because we collect no personal data through the App, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or port. If you have contacted us for support and would like your message deleted,
   email us at the address below and we will remove it.

  If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, California, or another jurisdiction with consumer privacy laws, you retain all rights granted by those laws.
  Where the law requires us to identify a legal basis for processing, our only basis is responding to a support request you initiated.

  ## Third parties

  We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to share.

  The App is distributed exclusively through the Mac App Store. Apple may collect anonymous aggregate analytics about App Store downloads, purchases, and crashes under its own
  privacy policy, which is independent of ours. You can review and control these analytics in **System Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements** on your Mac.

  ## Changes to this policy

  If we ever change how the App handles data, we will update this policy and revise the effective date above. For material changes, we will note the change clearly at the top of this
   document.
